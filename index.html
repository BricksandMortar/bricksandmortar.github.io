The Problem

Having a Church Management System Should Make Your Life Easier, Not Harder.

All you want is a central place to manage the life of your church. Unfortunately, although your member's information is there you can't quite access it in the way you want. Never mind the ancillary fees on top of your monthly payments.

If you're feeling frustrated with the struggle to get the information you need out of your system or the costs associated with your exisiting system are burning a hole in your pocket. You're not the only one.

It's time for change and we want to help deliver it. 

The Solution

We Build With Rock RMS

Rock RMS is an extravegently free open-source church management system that comes equipped with a powerful set of features to put your data to work for you and to save you money at the same time.

Don't believe us? Churches are switching in droves. See for yourself. 

How We Fit In

Make the Switch

Bricks and Mortar helps churches make the switch to Rock. From planning through to project management and implementation we've got the skills needed to get you up and running on Rock. We can help you figure out how your existing processes translate, create a framework that matches your church, and  then do the leg work to get you going.

Need custom features? No sweat. We've got that covered too.

  • Project Planning & Management

    Mapping out where to start with Rock can be hard. Using our knowledge of Rock we help map out the steps needed to get Rock operational and then implement them saving you time and energy that could be better devoted elsewhere. Now that's handy.

Custom Development

Sometimes what's in the box doesn't quite cut it. If that's the case for you then we can build in  the custom functionality you need to put Rock to work for you.

What We're About

Our Mission

We believe that the church is at it's most effective when it's able to respond to the needs of its community.  That's why our mission to provide churches with the tools and the time to do so. 

Alongside providing consultancy for churches we also produce plugins that will be available on the Rock Store (coming soon), contribute directly to Rock, and host the Shoulder the Boulder blog.

We believe in generosity.

That's why we make all of our plugins available for free on Github. They're free to download, install, and use. What's free now will be free tomorrow so there's no fear that we'll update a plugin and you'll be stuck on an older version or that a plugin will suddenly disappear.  

In addition we are committed to donating 10% of our profits to the Spark Development Network to support the ongoing development of Rock.
