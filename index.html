We build with Rock RMS

Rock RMS is an extravegently free open-source church management system that comes equipped with a powerful set of features. 
                  

  • Manage Families

    Store an infinite number of attributes about a person, visually represent information with dynamic badges, and securely storing notes about an individual.

  • Look After Groups

    Track group attendence, enable visitors to find your groups on your website, and give power to your group leaders.

  • Check-In Your Kids

    Keep track of attendence with check-in. It works on iOS, Windows, and any device with a web browser. 

  • Communicate

    Contact individuals via by SMS or email and add a touch of personality by using merging in stored information such as the recipients name and address

  • Create Custom Reports

    You're no longer restricted to fixed reports or no reports at all. Query your data and find out exactly how many 18-20s have attended in the past month or who in your children's team needs a background check.

  • Track Your Finance

    Take payments, record checks, and predict future giving. 

  • Power Your Website

    Power your website with data from Rock to allow users to donate, manage their information, and find groups. 

    • Secure Information

      Keep confidential information secure with Rock's security roles which allows every page and every piece of information to be locked down.

Customise

Rock's biggest strength is that Rock is extendible. If what you need is not there out of the box then don't worry. Rock has a powerful number of tools to mimic custom functionality.

Not powerful enough? Don't panic.
It's possible to add to or tweak nearly every aspect of Rock to make it fit your needs.
