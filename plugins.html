Our Take on Free

Blah blah blah

We understand that not all churches have an IT budget, or even an IT team. We also understand that sometimes the churches most that most need the tools are the churches that work with some of the poorest communities. That's why we offer our plugins on Github for free. They're free to download, install, and use. What's free now will be free tomorrow so there's no fear that we'll update a plugin and you'll be stuck on an older version or that a plugin will suddenly disappear.

Three Compelling Reasons to Buy

You

We produce plugins for sale in the Rock Store ranging from Slack integration to custom communication components. In addition, if you've got a need that isn't quite met by Rock or any existing plugins on the market we may just be the droids help you're looking for.

Us

Figuring out where to start with Rock can be hard. We've been there since pre-1.0 and we gotten several Rock t-shirts along the way. Whether it be a conversation about whether Rock is right for you, initial setup, project planning, post-setup tweaks, advanced configuration, or any combination of buzzwords you can think of we'd like to see if we can help.

The Community

We promise that if we don't feel like a project is a fit for us we won't waste your time, or ours. We don't charge for initial conversations and we won't charge five minute jobs. We promise to be affordable (in real terms) and we promise to be transparent every step of the way.
